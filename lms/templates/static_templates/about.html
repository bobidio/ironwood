Indigenous Inclusion

We are committed to include the Indigenous community with our business activities.

  • We are proud corporate partners of Tranby National Indigenous Adult Education and Training (Tranby). Tranby, is Australia’s longest running Aboriginal-controlled nationally registered training organisation (RTO), offering units of competency, full VET qualifications, and non-accredited skill development initiatives to Indigenous adults from across Australia. Tranby seeks to EDUCATE, ACHIEVE and EMPOWER their students and for over 60 years, they have seen graduates return to their communities empowered with newfound skills.

    Find out more about Tranby

  • Grosvenor are members of Supply Nation. Supply Nation works to connect over 1900 verified Indigenous businesses, along with procurement teams from government and the corporate community to help shape the Indigenous business sector. Supplier diversity is a growing movement in Australia and Grosvenor are committed to creating a more inclusive economy.

  • Indigenous Employment

    We offer an inclusive work environment and encourage Indigenous people to apply for positions within our organisation. Send your CV or apply for vacant positions advertised.
